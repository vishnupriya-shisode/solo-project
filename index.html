Top three Activitites to do in Sambhajinagar

Ajanta Caves, enjoy the old caves and sculptures and its rich history

The Ajanta Caves are a UNESCO World Heritage Site, famous for their ancient rock-cut caves, stunning sculptures, and beautiful Buddhist paintings carved into the hillside centuries ago.

Bibi Ka Maqbara, a beautiful Mughal mausoleum with intricate architecture

Often called the “Mini Taj Mahal,” Bibi Ka Maqbara is a graceful Mughal monument surrounded by gardens, showcasing elegant architecture and a peaceful atmosphere.

Paithan, a historic town known for its ancient temples and cultural heritage

Paithan is a historic town rich in culture, known for its ancient temples, traditional Paithani sarees, and deep roots in Indian history along the banks of the Godavari River.
